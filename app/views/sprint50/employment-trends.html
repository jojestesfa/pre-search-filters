Employment trends in your region

It is helpful to know what is happening in the world of work in your local area. This information can help you understand:

  • how employment rates compare to the rest of the country
  • how much people get paid in your area compared to the rest of the country
  • which sectors most jobs near you are in
  • which job sectors in your area are growing
  • how many hours people in your area work

Being aware of this information can help you make realistic career choices based on what’s happening in the world of work.

This information is available by region or by Local Enterprise Partnership (LEP). Regions are bigger and let you see what is happening in your area and surrounding areas. LEPs show smaller areas and this information is more specific to your local area.
