The Skills Toolkit

Learn new digital and numeracy skills at your own pace with a free online course using The Skills Toolkit.

Pick from a range of digital topics and skill levels, available from introductory to advanced.

Digital and numeracy skills are valuable and sought after for all kinds of jobs. 82% of all job vacancies require digital skills.

A digital course could help you:

  • exercise your mind during lockdown or furlough (temporary leave)
  • progress your career
  • explore new job prospects
  • get started online
