Job group: Youth and community workers

Youth and community workers provide support to individuals or groups of individuals through a range of activities or services that aim to encourage participation in social and community life and promote personal and social development.

How this information can help you

If you know what is happening in the labour market, it can help you to make realistic decisions about:

  • what you want to do in your career
  • what industry you want to work in
  • how much you need to earn
  • where you want to work
  • qualifications and training you’ll need to do your job

The information on this page will give you the facts you need to help you make those decisions.

Future job growth

This information tells you whether jobs in a sector are likely to go up or down in the future.

Top industries in this job group

Find out which sectors or industries are the biggest employers of the job you are interested in. This can help you look for vacancies in the right places.

Employment by region

See which areas of the country have the most opportunities for the job you want to do. It can help you weigh up whether you're likely to get a job near home or if you could improve your chances by moving or travelling to another area.

Qualification level

Explore the typical qualification level held by people in this job group. This can help you to see what sorts of skills and training you might need.

Unemployment rate

A high unemployment rate can mean that there aren’t many jobs available and that it could be difficult for you to find work in this area. The lower the unemployment rate is, the easier it should be to find a job in this field.

Employment status

This information can help you see what the most common work patterns are in this job group. You can get an idea of how likely it is that you could work full-time, part-time, employed or on a self-employed basis in your chosen sector.

Gender breakdown

Exploring the numbers of men and women in a particular sector can tell you about issues of equality and any barriers you might face at work. It may help you to think about how you will overcome them.
